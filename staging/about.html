OUR STORY

WE SAFEGUARD THE ENVIRONMENT
SINCE 1998

We give solutions and services mainly in the field of environment and all types of pollution control. We provide consultancy for entire gamut of Environmental Management System. We undertake turnkey projects consisting of engineering design, manufacturing, installation, commissioning, troubleshooting and training of all types of raw water, domestic and industrial wastewater treatment, air pollution control and solid waste management systems.

Since our inception, we have been following ethical business principles. We have always strived to improvise quality of our products and services with consistent industry in-depth analysis, as it is directly proportionate to the satisfaction of our valuable customers. Our commitment for excellence and passion for quality products and services have shown us the way to growth and prosperity.

Our Strength. We have a highly committed workforce headed by skilled professionals who continuously raise to the expectations of the industry standards by running that extra mile, which distinguishes us from others.
