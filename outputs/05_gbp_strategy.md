## GBP Analysis for Industrial B2B

### Key Insight
GBP is typically for local service businesses, but for industrial B2B:
- Use for **local SEO signals** (maps ranking)
- Share **project completions** (social proof)
- Post **industry insights** (thought leadership)
- **Don't** post daily - 2-4/month is sufficient
